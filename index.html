Hello! I'm Kelly

I am a geographer turned data journalist, and an occasional photographer, based in New York.

Before coming to journalism, I worked at a remote sensing company, analyzing measurements of methane using satellite technology. I love anything involving geospatial analysis or mapping, and am excited to use those tools to find and tell news stories.

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Data Journalism at Columbia Journalism School.
