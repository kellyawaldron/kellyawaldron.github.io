Hi, I'm Kelly.


I am a French/Irish data journalist, and an occasional photographer, based in New York City. I am currently pursuing a Masters in Data Journalism at Columbia Journalism School.


Before coming to journalism, I studied geography and worked at a remote sensing company. I love anything involving spatial analysis or mapping, and am excited to use those tools to find news stories.
